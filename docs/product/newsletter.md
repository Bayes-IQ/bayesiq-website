# BayesIQ Newsletter — Product Source of Truth

## What It Is

A low-frequency email list for data and analytics practitioners who want to stay sharp on telemetry validation, metric integrity, and data quality engineering. Not a sales drip. Not a product changelog.

## What Subscribers Receive

- **Telemetry audit tips** — specific techniques for validating event schemas, tracking plans, and pipeline contracts
- **Checklists** — practical, copy-paste-ready lists for audit prep, metric review cadences, and schema governance
- **Lightweight case notes** — anonymised examples of broken metrics we've encountered: what broke, why, and what fixed it
- **Occasional announcements** — new services, self-assessment tools, or resources when they genuinely add value

## Cadence

Roughly twice a month. We send when we have something worth sending, not on a fixed schedule. Subscribers will not receive daily or weekly emails.

## Consent Language

The following wording is used in the signup form and must remain aligned with the `/privacy` policy:

> "Subscribe for telemetry tips, audit checklists, and short case notes. No spam — unsubscribe any time. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy)."

Rules:
- No pre-checked opt-in boxes
- Email address only — do not collect name, company, or other fields at signup
- Explicit unsubscribe language always present
- Link to `/privacy` always present
